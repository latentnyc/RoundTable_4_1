# Conditions Reference

## Overview
When an entity is affected by a condition, narrate its visual and mechanical effects in-character.

## Condition Descriptions
- **Blinded**: The creature cannot see. Describe darkness, blurred shapes, or struggling to locate foes.
- **Stunned / Paralyzed**: The creature is frozen, incapacitated, or unable to move. Describe stiff limbs, blank stares, or electric/magic locking them in place.
- **Poisoned / Sickened**: The creature is weakened. Describe pale skin, vomiting, trembling hands, or breathing heavily.
- **Frightened**: The creature is terrified. Describe wide eyes, backing away, or shaking with fear.
- **Prone**: The creature has been knocked flat on the ground. Describe them falling, scrambling in the dirt, or struggling to stand up.
- **Grappled / Restrained**: The creature is held in place. Describe vines wrapping them, a firm grip, or heavy chains pinning their arms.
