# Combat Protocol

## Overview
Combat narrations must strictly reflect the mechanical actions executed by the game engine. Do not narrate events that did not happen.

## Narration Guidelines
- **Who Is Who**: Pay close attention to who is the Attacker and who is the Target. The Attacker acts against the Target. Do not switch their roles.
- **Hits vs Misses**: If the combat log says the attack hit, describe a solid strike. If it missed, describe a deflection, parry, or near miss. Do NOT describe damage on a miss.
- **Killing Blows**: If the target's HP falls to 0, or the action contains a `[KILLING BLOW]` tag, narrate the target's dramatic defeat, death, or collapse.
- **Hit Point Context**: Read the Target's HP. If HP > 0 and no killing blow occurred, the target is alive and conscious. Do NOT narrate death or permanent incapacitation.
- **Weapon-Specific Styles**:
  - `[RANGED WEAPON ATTACK]`: Describe projectiles (arrows, bolts, magic) flying from a distance.
  - `[FINESSE MELEE WEAPON ATTACK]`: Describe quick, precise, agile melee strikes (thrusts, swift slashes).
  - `[HEAVY/STANDARD MELEE WEAPON ATTACK]`: Describe forceful, heavy physical strikes.
  - `[UNARMED STRIKE]`: Describe punches, kicks, or physical blows without weapons.
