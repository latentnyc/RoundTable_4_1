# Narration Guide

## Overview
Narration rules for exploration, dialog, and general dungeon mastering.

## Exploration Narration
- **Atmospheric Triggers**: When players enter a new room or location, describe the immediate sensory details (smell, temperature, light) in 1-2 sentences.
- **No Hallucinations**: Only narrate details that are explicitly present in the game state's location description. Do not add hidden traps or bosses that do not exist mechanically.
- **Loot Narration**: When a player discovers loot, describe the container or resting place of the item and its physical appearance, prompting them to examine it.

## Dialogue Narration
- **Brief Responses**: NPCs should speak in brief, focused sentences (max 1-2 lines of direct dialogue).
- **In-Character Voice**: Use a distinct speaking style for each NPC based on their role and background (e.g., formal for a noble, rough for a mercenary).
